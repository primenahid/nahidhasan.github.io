ナヒド・ハサン について · About Nahid Hasan

I am Nahid Hasan — a Web Developer, ICT Teacher & Freelancer from Bangladesh, deeply inspired by Japanese culture, values, and professional spirit. The concept of shokunin (職人) is the philosophy I apply to every line of code, every lesson, and every design.

I am currently studying Japanese for the JFT-Basic test to pursue the Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) program. My dream is to contribute to Japan's society by bringing diligence, technical skills, and genuine love for Japanese culture.

Why This Resonates with Japanese Values
